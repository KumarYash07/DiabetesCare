About DiabetesCare

DiabetesCare is a non-profit initiative dedicated to providing accessible, reliable information and tools for diabetes awareness and prevention. Our mission is to empower individuals to take proactive steps towards a healthier life free from the burdens of diabetes.

Our Vision

A world where every person has the knowledge and resources to prevent, manage, and ultimately overcome diabetes.

Our Features

  • Informational Resources: Comprehensive articles on the causes, symptoms, and prevention of diabetes.
  • Risk Assessment Tool: A custom machine learning model, trained on public health data, to provide an estimated risk score for Type 2 Diabetes.
  • Community Focus: A platform to connect users with resources and support (future feature).
